SO, WHAT IS IT?

A Growth Mindset is learning to harness the power of not yet. It’s believing that with enough effort you can persevere and overcome challenges to develop new skills and intelligence, and further improve them as well. A Growth Mindset is about focusing on the process. The length of the road doesn’t matter, how long it takes you to take a step forward doesn’t matter, what matters is the belief that with hard work and effort, you can always improve. A Growth Mindset is seeing failure as nothing more than an opportunity to learn and get better.

GROWTH MINDSET

SKILLS + INTELLIGENCE

Can be developed.

MAIN CONCERN

Learning / Getting better. Focus on process/progress.

EFFORT

An important part of learning.

CHALLENGES

Persevere / Work through it. Show more GRIT.

FEEDBACK

Like it / Use it to learn.

MISTAKES

Treat them as a learning opportunity.

FIXED MINDSET

SKILLS + INTELLIGENCE

Set. You have what you have.

MAIN CONCERN

How they look. Focus on performance.

EFFORT

Something you do when you are not good.

CHALLENGES

Give-up / Check out.

FEEDBACK

Take it personally / Get defensive.

MISTAKES

Hate them / Try to avoid making them.
